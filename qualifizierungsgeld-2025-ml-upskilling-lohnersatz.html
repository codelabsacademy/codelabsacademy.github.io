Want to boost your company's skills and cut training costs? Germany's new Weiterbildungsgesetz offers a fantastic opportunity!

Government Funding for Upskilling

The law provides up to 67% reimbursement for employee training in machine learning. That's a huge incentive to invest in your team's future. Imagine the possibilities!

How it Works

Companies can get financial support for sending their staff on machine learning courses. This helps employees learn new skills and stay competitive. It's a win-win!

Who Benefits?

Any business looking to improve its workforce's machine learning skills can benefit. It's a great way to attract and retain talent. Plus, it's a smart financial move.

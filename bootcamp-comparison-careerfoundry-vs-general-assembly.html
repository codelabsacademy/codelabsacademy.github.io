CareerFoundry vs. General Assembly

Two popular names keep popping up: CareerFoundry and General Assembly. Both offer intensive courses, but they have different strengths. Let's break it down.

What's the Difference?

CareerFoundry focuses on online, self-paced learning. It's great for flexibility. General Assembly offers both online and in-person options. You get more direct interaction with instructors.

Which Bootcamp is Right for You?

It depends on your learning style and preferences. Do you prefer the structure of a classroom? Or the freedom of learning at your own pace? Consider your budget too. Prices vary.

Making Your Decision

Read reviews. Talk to current students or alumni. Research the specific courses offered by each bootcamp. Choosing the right bootcamp is a big decision. Take your time and do your homework. Good luck!
